Processing Technology

How it Works

Our approved and state-licensed facility uses advanced methodologies, and an expert team to deliver pure, safe, and accurately-dosed cannabis concentrates and innovative extracts.

Hydrocarbon Extraction

Noble labs is devoted to ensuring that our extraction process creates a final product that is a clean consistent and quality concentration of the cannabinoids, terpenes, and flavonoids that make cannabis such an effective medicine. By utilizing hydrocarbon extraction technology, we are able to safely extract these desirable components while leaving the unwanted constituents behind in the starting material. The resulting extract can then be further refined, distilled, and isolated depending on end product goals, and is in line with our goal of creating an end product that is a faithful representation of what makes cannabis great.

Cannabinoid Distillation

Our system provides easy solutions to turn quality "trim" into high-purity cannabinoid distillates. This is the cutting edge of cannabinoid distillation, extraction, and purification techniques, which enable us to create a final product that can be used in a variety of value-added cannabis products.

Terpene Extraction

This process allows us to utilize hydrocarbon technology to extract cannabis-sourced terpenoid compounds from the starting biomass material. This distinguisher allows us to offer patients the ability to utilize the "entourage effect.”

Who’s eligible to be recommended and receive medical marijuana in Mississippi?

During the 2022 legislative session, the passage of S. B. No. 2095 made Mississippi the 37th state to legalize marijuana. Physicians, certified nurse practitioner, physician assistants or optometrists can be licensed to prescribe medical marijuana. Distribution in Mississippi

Within state-sanctioned programs nationwide, Mississippi has positioned itself to offer a very robust marijuana program. Having no caps on licensing, along with a pro small-business tier model, one can expect to see easy access, competitive pricing and a healthy supply of medicinal varietals.
